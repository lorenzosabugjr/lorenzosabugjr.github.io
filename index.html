Short Bio

I am a doctoral candidate at Politecnico di Milano, working under supervision of prof. Lorenzo Fagiano and prof. Fredy Ruiz. I am particularly interested in data-driven optimization algorithms, with applications to UAVs and other aerospace systems. I earned a M.Sc. from RWTH Aachen, and have performed research in non-linear and multi-agent control for HVDC power systems. After my M.Sc. I have worked on a radio payload currently onboard the microsatellite Diwata-2.
