Short Bio

I am a doctoral candidate at Politecnico di Milano. I am particularly interested in non-linear and distributed control, with applications to spacecraft and other aerospace systems. I earned a M.Sc. from RWTH Aachen, and have performed research in non-linear and multi-agent control for HVDC power systems. After my M.Sc. I have worked on a radio payload currently onboard the microsatellite Diwata-2.
